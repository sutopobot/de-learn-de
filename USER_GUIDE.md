## ğŸ’¡ Learning Tips

### 1. Daily Practice
- **15-30 minutes** per day is better than 3 hours once a week
- Set a reminder!

### 2. Use TTS (Text-to-Speech)
- Listen to every word/phrase
- Repeat out loud
- Train your ear and mouth

### 3. Focus on Gender Early
- German gender is random - memorize with the noun!
- Always learn "der Tisch" not just "Tisch"

### 4. Make Mistakes
- Learn Mode is for mistakes
- Better to fail now than on exam day

### 5. Track Progress
- Check your streak
- Review wrong answers
- Celebrate small wins!
